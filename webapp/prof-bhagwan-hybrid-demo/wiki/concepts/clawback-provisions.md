# Clawback Provisions

Contractual terms contingent on an observable signal that can be embedded in contracts to lower or eliminate rents from hidden information. Contractual provisions on managerial compensation that can reduce or eliminate the cost of hidden information, but may be crowded out by high equilibrium pay levels. A contractual innovation, often observed for PE fund managers, that is triggered if a fund's realized IRR at its closing is less than a pre-specified benchmark.
