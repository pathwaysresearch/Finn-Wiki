# Time Diversification

A concept that focuses on managing the idiosyncratic volatility of a portfolio over time, rather than just across different assets at a single point in time. A form of volatility management that involves adjusting a portfolio based on how risks are changing over time, which affects and can reduce convexity costs. A portfolio management concept that emphasizes managing risk over time, recognizing that investors only have one run of time.
