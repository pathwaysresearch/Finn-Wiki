# Capital Invested in Existing Assets

The amount of capital deployed in a firm's current operational assets, crucial for evaluating their quality. While market value includes future growth, book value of capital is often used as a proxy, though it requires extensive adjustments for historical accounting decisions like depreciation and inventory valuation.
