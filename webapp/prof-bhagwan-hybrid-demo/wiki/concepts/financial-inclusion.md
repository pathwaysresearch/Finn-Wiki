# Financial Inclusion

A drive to make financial services accessible to all, particularly the poor, which the text argues has not yet been meaningfully achieved in India despite high numbers of bank accounts. The effort to make financial products and services accessible to all, which the text argues has not been fully successful for its intended recipients like the BOP group. The effort to make formal financial services accessible to underserved populations, addressing barriers such as low digital literacy and the difficulty of simple tasks like checking a bank balance.
