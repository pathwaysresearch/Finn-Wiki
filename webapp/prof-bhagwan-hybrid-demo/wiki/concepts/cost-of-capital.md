# Cost of Capital

The required, risk-adjusted discount rate (r) for a company's cash flows, which is composed of the dividend yield (D/P) and the implicit rate of growth (g). A crucial number for a firm's decision-making that represents the opportunity cost of investing, and is also referred to as the rate of return from an investor's perspective.
