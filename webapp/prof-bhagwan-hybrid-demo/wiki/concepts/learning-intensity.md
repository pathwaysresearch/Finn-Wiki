# Learning Intensity

A parameter (s) representing how much organization capital is built per dollar of initial investment, making the date-0 investment more productive. The rate of cost reduction per unit of cumulative output, where firms in industries with steeper learning curves are predicted to enter new markets at a larger initial scale.
