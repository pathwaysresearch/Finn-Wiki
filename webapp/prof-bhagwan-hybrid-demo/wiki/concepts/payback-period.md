# Payback Period

A capital budgeting method that determines the minimum number of periods required for the sum of future cash flows to exceed the initial investment, and is criticized for ignoring the time value of money. A capital budgeting criterion that was the most popular method used by large US firms as of a survey from the mid-2000s.
