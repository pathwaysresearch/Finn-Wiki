# Tail Risk

The risk associated with the tails of the distribution of returns, representing the possibility of losing a lot of money or missing a major opportunity, which is a key focus in compounding. The risk of extreme negative outcomes in a portfolio's return distribution, which are considered to have the most important effect on its final value.
