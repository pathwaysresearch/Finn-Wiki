# Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) Valuation

A general valuation method that involves estimating future cash flows and discounting them back to the present. The text discusses two main ways to adjust for risk within DCF: adjusting the discount rate or adjusting the cash flows themselves (certainty equivalents). It is described as the most commonly used approach.
