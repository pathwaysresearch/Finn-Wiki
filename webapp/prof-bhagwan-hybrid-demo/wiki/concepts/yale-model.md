# The Yale Model

A non-traditional investment approach for an endowment fund that emphasizes understanding the unique characteristics of the institution, such as its long time horizon, rather than being a replicable formula for individuals. An investment model that begins its process by addressing fundamental questions about every characteristic of an investment.
