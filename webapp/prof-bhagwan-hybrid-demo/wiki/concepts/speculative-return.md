# Speculative Return

A component of market returns in Bogle's framework, defined as the impact of changes in market valuation (such as P/E ratios) on the fundamental investment return. A component of total market return derived from the change in the price-to-earnings (P/E) ratio, which Bogle notes tends to revert to the mean over long periods.
