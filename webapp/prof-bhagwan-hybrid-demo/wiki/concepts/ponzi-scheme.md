# Ponzi Scheme

A type of fraudulent scheme that targets people, including those from lower-income groups, with promises of spectacular returns that ultimately result in financial loss. A fraudulent investment scheme that lures in the poor with promises of unprecedented returns, pays some high figures to consolidate trust, and then snatches the principal away entirely.
