# Impact Investing

A theory where socially minded investors hold large ownership stakes in socially valuable firms to incentivize for-profit owners to pursue social goals, especially when owners cannot commit to those objectives. A practice where socially conscious investors purchase financial claims in for-profit firms to incentivize owners to pursue social goals, which is consistent with the common SRI practice of targeted financial investments.
