# Caricature Effect

An identity lock-in that accumulates with organization capital, raising the effective opportunity cost of redeployment and inhibiting innovation by locking resources into predetermined strategic roles. An organizational trap where a firm's hardened identity leads to social sanctioning of individuals who advocate for identity-disrupting innovations.
