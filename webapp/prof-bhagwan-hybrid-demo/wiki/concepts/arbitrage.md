# Arbitrage

A transaction that exploits a violation of the Law of One Price to generate a risk-free profit with no initial investment of personal capital. A riskless "free lunch" transaction that costs no money down, generates immediate cash flow, and requires no future payments, often identified through inconsistencies in pricing models.
