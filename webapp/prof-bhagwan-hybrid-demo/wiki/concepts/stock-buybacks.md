# Stock Buybacks

A method increasingly used by firms to return cash to stockholders, serving as an alternative to dividends. Their growing prevalence challenges traditional dividend discount models, but they can be incorporated into valuation by expanding the definition of dividends to include them, often through a modified payout ratio. Buybacks are often less smooth than dividends.
