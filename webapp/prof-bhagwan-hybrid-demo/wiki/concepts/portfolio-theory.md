# Portfolio Theory

A systematic approach to making investment decisions that provides an economic logic for investing, rather than trying to pick a single winning stock or 'beat the market'. An academic framework that determines what an investor should do under the assumption that stock prices are random and perfectly priced, with the goal of achieving higher return and lower risk.
