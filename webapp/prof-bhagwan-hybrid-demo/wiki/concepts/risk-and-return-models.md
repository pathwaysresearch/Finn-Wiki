# Risk and Return Models

A practical approach to converting uncertain cash flows into certainty equivalents, or for estimating risk premiums for risk-adjusted discount rates. These models use the same approach to estimate risk premiums for both methods. They are considered more practical than using utility functions for certainty equivalents.
