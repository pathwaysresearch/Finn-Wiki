# Shadow cost of public funds

A parameter (λ) representing the societal cost of raising government funds, where λ > 1 indicates an excess burden from taxation. A parameter (λ) representing the welfare cost of raising public funds through taxation, which makes fiscally-funded mechanisms like prizes less attractive when λ > 1.
