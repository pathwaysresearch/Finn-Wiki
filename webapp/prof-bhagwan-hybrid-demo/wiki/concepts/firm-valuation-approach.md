# Firm Valuation Approach

A valuation method that focuses on the cash flows available to the entire firm (FCFF) before any debt payments. It requires information about debt ratios and interest rates to estimate the Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC) for discounting. This approach should yield the same equity value as the equity valuation approach if consistent assumptions about financial leverage are made.
