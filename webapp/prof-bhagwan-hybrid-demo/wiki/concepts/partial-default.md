# Partial Default

A situation where a sovereign borrower discharges the overhang of debt through mechanisms like debt-equity swaps or rescheduling, allowing them to seek fresh financing without total repudiation. A situation where a borrower does not fully repudiate their debt, which is noted as being more numerous in the history of international lending than complete repudiation.
