# Discounted Cash Flow Valuation Models

Valuation models that estimate value by discounting future cash flows. While they should implicitly link reinvestment and growth, analysts often estimate these as separate inputs. These models can converge with excess return models when consistent assumptions about growth and reinvestment are applied.
