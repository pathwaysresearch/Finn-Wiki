# Stable Matching

The equilibrium concept for the matching market, defined as a matching that is individually rational and cannot be blocked by any firm-manager pair. A matching of managers to firms where no manager-firm pair would prefer to match with each other over their current partners, with multiple such matchings possible in a balanced market. A matching of firms and managers where no pair has an incentive to break their current matches and form a new partnership with each other, thereby blocking the arrangement. A matching between firms and managers where no firm-manager pair has an incentive to deviate and form a new partnership that is better for both.
