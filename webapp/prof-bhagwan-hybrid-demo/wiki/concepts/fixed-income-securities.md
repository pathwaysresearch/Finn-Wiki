# Fixed Income Securities

A class of securities central to the market dislocations of the time, whose valuation can be determined using present value techniques without considering uncertainty. Financial instruments that have clearly stated payoffs that are fixed and known in advance, in contrast to stocks which have uncertain cash flows.
