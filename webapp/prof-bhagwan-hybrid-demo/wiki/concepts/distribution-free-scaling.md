# Distribution-Free Scaling

A theoretical result demonstrating that the fixed-target semivariance of a reported series ($LPM_{2,τ}(Y)$) is equal to the true series' semivariance ($LPM_{2,τ}(X)$) multiplied by $(1 – ε)²$, a relationship that holds for any distribution.
