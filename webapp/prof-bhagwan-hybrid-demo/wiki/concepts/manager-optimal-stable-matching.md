# Manager-Optimal Stable Matching

The stable matching that yields the highest possible payoffs for managers, resulting in widespread pay escalation and firms shifting to the growth regime. A specific stable matching arrangement that provides the highest possible payoffs to the managers compared to any other stable matching.
