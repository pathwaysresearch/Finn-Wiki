# Extended Equity Valuation Models

Models designed to address the limitations of the traditional dividend discount model by capturing the build-up of cash in a firm's value. They consider methods other than just dividends for returning cash to stockholders, such as stock buybacks. These models aim to provide a more comprehensive valuation by reflecting all forms of cash returned to shareholders.
