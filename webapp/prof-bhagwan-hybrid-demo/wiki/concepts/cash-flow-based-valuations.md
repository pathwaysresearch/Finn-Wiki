# Cash Flow Based Valuations

Valuation methods that consider a company's actual cash flows, which can ebb and flow with earnings and reinvestments. These are contrasted with dividend-based valuations, as dividends tend to be more stable than cash flows. Unlike dividend discount models, they account for the full cash generating capacity of a firm.
