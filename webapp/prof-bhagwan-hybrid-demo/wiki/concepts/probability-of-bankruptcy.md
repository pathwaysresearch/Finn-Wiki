# Probability of Bankruptcy

The likelihood of a firm entering bankruptcy, which can be estimated indirectly through bond ratings and their associated empirical default probabilities, or via statistical approaches based on observable firm characteristics. This probability is a key factor in assessing expected bankruptcy costs.
