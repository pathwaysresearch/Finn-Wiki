# Tangency Portfolio

The single, specific risky portfolio on the efficient frontier that, when combined with a risk-free asset, provides the best possible risk-reward opportunities for all investors. The optimal portfolio of risky assets that, when combined with a risk-free asset, provides the highest possible risk-reward trade-off as measured by the Sharpe ratio. A unique portfolio on the efficient frontier, labeled 'M', which all investors would hold in combination with a risk-free asset to achieve their desired risk-reward profile. The market portfolio which, by assumption, cannot have negative weights and is used as the benchmark for measuring an asset's covariability.
