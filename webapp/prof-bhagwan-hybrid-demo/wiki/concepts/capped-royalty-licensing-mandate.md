# Capped Royalty Licensing Mandate (CRLM)

A mechanism that achieves innovation and access by mandating an innovator to license generic production in poor countries subject to an optimal per-unit royalty cap. A proposed policy mechanism that treats an innovator's profit as an instrument to balance innovation and access, presented as an alternative to patents and prizes.
