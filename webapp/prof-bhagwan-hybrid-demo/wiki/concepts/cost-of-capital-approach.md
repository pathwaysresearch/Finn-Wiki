# Cost of Capital Approach

A valuation method where the effects of leverage, including tax benefits and bankruptcy costs, are incorporated into the discount rate (cost of capital). The tax benefit is in the after-tax cost of debt, and bankruptcy costs are in the levered beta and pre-tax cost of debt. This approach is often contrasted with the Adjusted Present Value approach.
