# Yield Curve

A plot of interest rates as a function of time, also known as the term structure of interest rates, which gives a sense of where future interest rates are expected to go. A plot that shows the average interest rate (yield) across various different maturities for a set of securities, whose shape indicates market expectations for future interest rates. A representation of the term structure of interest rates, which can be upward-sloping, backward-bending, or inverted.
