# Nonprofit Status

A corporate structure that can be optimal for projects with a very high desired level of social attention, particularly when frictions like corporate taxes for for-profit firms are a concern. A corporate structure where the impact investor holds the full residual cash flow claim, which the paper argues is a suboptimal arrangement for achieving social goals.
