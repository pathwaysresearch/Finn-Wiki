# Certainty Equivalent Models

An alternative approach within DCF valuation where expected cash flows are adjusted for risk, rather than adjusting the discount rate. This process replaces uncertain expected cash flows with certainty equivalent cash flows. The text notes that if risk premiums from risk and return models are used, this approach yields the same value as risk-adjusted discount rates.
