# Modified Dividend Payout Ratio

A financial ratio that expands the definition of dividends to include stock buybacks, calculated as (Dividends + Stock buybacks) / Net income. This ratio can be further adjusted by netting out new debt issued if buybacks are used to increase financial leverage. Using an average over several years is recommended due to the unsmoothed nature of buybacks.
