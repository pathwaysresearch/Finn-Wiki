# Cost of Equity

The rate used to discount expected dividends in valuation models like the Dividend Discount Model and the Gordon Growth Model. It represents the return required by equity investors for bearing the risk of holding the company's stock. The Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM) is one method used to estimate this cost.
