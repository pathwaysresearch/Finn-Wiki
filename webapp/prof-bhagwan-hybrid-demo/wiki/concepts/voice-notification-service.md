# Voice Notification Service

A transparency mechanism using voice calls to confirm transaction history and bank balances, studied as an intervention to improve knowledge, trust, and account usage among low-income women in India. A service that provides an audible confirmation of a digital payment, helping merchants verify that a money transfer has occurred and preventing certain types of scams.
