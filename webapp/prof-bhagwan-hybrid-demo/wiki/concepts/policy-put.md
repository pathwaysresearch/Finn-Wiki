# Policy Put

A mechanism of legitimate intervention, such as countercyclical fiscal policy, that compresses true outcomes before reporting and typically compresses both tails of the distribution symmetrically. A genuine economic intervention that transforms a true outcome into a smoother realized outcome, which is then reported honestly, creating an observational equivalence with a reporting put. A legitimate stabilization policy that serves as a confounder for misreporting tests, as it cannot be distinguished from manipulation by C_p or C_o without external data.
