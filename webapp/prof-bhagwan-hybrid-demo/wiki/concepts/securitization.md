# Securitization

A financial innovation where borrowing is done directly from financial markets by pooling assets, rather than through traditional intermediaries like banks. A financial engineering process that repackages identical securities into different assets, like senior and junior tranches, to create securities with varying risk profiles that appeal to different investors.
