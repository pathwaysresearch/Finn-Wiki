# Lindahl Royalty

The optimal royalty cap used in the Capped Royalty Licensing Mandate, determined by the Lindahl conditions for efficiency. The socially optimal royalty in the CRLM framework that achieves the first-best innovation level by having poor countries' payments fill the gap between the optimal prize and rich countries' contributions.
