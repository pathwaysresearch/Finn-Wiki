# Compressed APV Approach

A variation of the Adjusted Present Value (APV) model that uses capital cash flows, which include tax benefits from interest payments. In this approach, tax savings from debt are discounted at the unlevered cost of equity, and expected bankruptcy costs are effectively ignored. It is argued to be simpler for levered transactions with changing leverage.
