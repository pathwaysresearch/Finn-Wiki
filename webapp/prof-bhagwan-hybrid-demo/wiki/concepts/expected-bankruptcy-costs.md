# Expected Bankruptcy Costs

The costs associated with a firm's financial distress or bankruptcy, which reduce firm value. These include direct costs (e.g., legal fees) and significant indirect costs (e.g., lost customers, higher employee turnover, tighter supplier restrictions) that can range from 10% to 25% of firm value. These costs are subtracted in the Adjusted Present Value (APV) approach.
