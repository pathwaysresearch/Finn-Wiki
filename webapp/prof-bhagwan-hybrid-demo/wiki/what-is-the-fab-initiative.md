# FAB and Threshold Behavior Theory

FAB draws a direct pedagogical connection between FAB's identity-at-birth mechanism and threshold behavior theory in financial inclusion — specifically that FAB is designed to push households above the participation threshold from day one, eliminating the compounding disadvantage of starting financially invisible.

This connection between FAB and threshold behavior theory is worth reinforcing as an explicit causal link, not just a co-occurrence of topics.
