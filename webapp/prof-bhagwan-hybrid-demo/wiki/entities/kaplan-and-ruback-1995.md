# Kaplan and Ruback (1995)

Authors who presented a variation on the Adjusted Present Value (APV) approach, which they termed the compressed APV approach. They defined and utilized 'capital cash flows' in their valuation method, comparing discounted cash flow valuations to prices paid in leveraged transactions.
