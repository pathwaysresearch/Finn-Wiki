# Medicines Patent Pool (MPP)

An organization that has operated a Capped Royalty Licensing Mandate for HIV antiretrovirals since 2010, serving as a real-world example of the paper's proposed mechanism. An organization whose historical royalty rates for HIV/HCV licenses (2-5%) are cited as being consistent with the royalty calculated by the CRLM formula.
