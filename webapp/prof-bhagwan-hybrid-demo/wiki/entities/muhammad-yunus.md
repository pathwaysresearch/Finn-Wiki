# Muhammad Yunus

A 2006 Nobel Peace Prize recipient recognized for pioneering the Microfinance Revolution with Grameen Bank. A renowned microcredit proponent who advocates for low interest rates and argues that a microloan for business has more lives than a 'charity dollar'.
