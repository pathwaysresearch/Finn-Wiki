# UPI (Unified Payments Interface)

A payment system in India that has experienced a significant surge in QR code-based transactions, but for which the dispute resolution process lacks credibility among the rural population. A payment system whose meteoric growth is used as an example to demonstrate the enormous returns and positive externalities of investing in digital financial infrastructure.
