# PMJDY

A government scheme in India (Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana) that, by being linked to Aadhaar and its associated benefits, discourages borrowers from defaulting on small loans. A successful government drive under which banks opened accounts for all unbanked people in an area.
