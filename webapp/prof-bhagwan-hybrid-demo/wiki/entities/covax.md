# COVAX

A real-world initiative cited as a large-scale confirmation of the theory that voluntary prize funds for global public goods are chronically under-provided due to free-rider problems. A real-world collective purchase initiative cited as an example of under-provision, having raised only a fraction of the estimated optimal prize for a vaccine.
