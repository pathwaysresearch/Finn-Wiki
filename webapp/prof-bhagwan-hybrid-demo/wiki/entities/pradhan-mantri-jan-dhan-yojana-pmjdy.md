# Pradhan Mantri Jan-Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)

A national financial inclusion mission in India that is presented as having failed to enroll eligible people in benefits like life insurance due to complex eligibility criteria. A government scheme launched in 2014 that prompted individuals, such as the shopkeeper Ajit Pal, to open a bank account. A financial inclusion programme launched by the Government of India in 2014 to provide access to financial services like savings accounts and credit to all Indian citizens, particularly the poor.
