# Nicholas Bernoulli

An economist who proposed a hypothetical gamble, known as the St. Petersburg Paradox, in one of the most widely cited thought experiments in economics. He also unveiled the notion of a certainty equivalent in the context of this paradox, arguing its relevance for risk-averse investors.
