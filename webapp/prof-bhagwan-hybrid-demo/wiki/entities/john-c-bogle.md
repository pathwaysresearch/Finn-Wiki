# John C. Bogle

A key figure in the mutual fund industry who wrote an influential senior thesis, worked at and was later fired from Wellington Fund, and developed early thoughts on indexation. The founder of Vanguard who created the first index fund and developed concepts like the Cost Matters Hypothesis and the Sources of Market Returns framework. An investor who advocates for creating reasonable expectations, distinguishes between investing and speculating, and personally holds a 50/50 stock-bond portfolio.
