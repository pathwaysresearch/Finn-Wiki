# Reserve Bank of India (RBI)

India's central bank, where the term "financial inclusion" was officially introduced by its former governor Y.V. Reddy. The institution that launched the Integrated Ombudsman Scheme in late 2021 to provide a more efficient way for consumers to address financial grievances.
